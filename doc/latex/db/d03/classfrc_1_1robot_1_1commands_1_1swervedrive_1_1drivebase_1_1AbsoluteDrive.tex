Used to drive a swerve robot in full field-\/centric mode. 

vX and vY supply translation inputs, where x is torwards/away from alliance wall and y is left/right. heading\+Horzontal and heading\+Vertical are the Cartesian coordinates from which the robot\textquotesingle{}s angle will be derivedâ€” they will be converted to a polar angle, which the robot will rotate to.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em swerve} & The swerve drivebase subsystem. \\
\hline
{\em vX} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the x-\/translation joystick input. Should be in the range -\/1 to 1 with deadband already accounted for. Positive X is away from the alliance wall. \\
\hline
{\em vY} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the y-\/translation joystick input. Should be in the range -\/1 to 1 with deadband already accounted for. Positive Y is towards the left wall when looking through the driver station glass. \\
\hline
{\em heading\+Horizontal} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the horizontal component of the robot\textquotesingle{}s heading angle. In the robot coordinate system, this is along the same axis as vY. Should range from -\/1 to 1 with no deadband. Positive is towards the left wall when looking through the driver station glass. \\
\hline
{\em heading\+Vertical} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the vertical component of the robot\textquotesingle{}s heading angle. In the robot coordinate system, this is along the same axis as vX. Should range from -\/1 to 1 with no deadband. Positive is away from the alliance wall. \\
