Used to drive a swerve robot in full field-\/centric mode. 

vX and vY supply translation inputs, where x is torwards/away from alliance wall and y is left/right. Heading Adjust changes the current heading after being multipied by a constant. The look booleans are shortcuts to get the robot to face a certian direction. Based off of ideas in \href{https://www.chiefdelphi.com/t/experiments-with-a-swerve-steering-knob/446172}{\texttt{ https\+://www.\+chiefdelphi.\+com/t/experiments-\/with-\/a-\/swerve-\/steering-\/knob/446172}}


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em swerve} & The swerve drivebase subsystem. \\
\hline
{\em vX} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the x-\/translation joystick input. Should be in the range -\/1 to 1 with deadband already accounted for. Positive X is away from the alliance wall. \\
\hline
{\em vY} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the y-\/translation joystick input. Should be in the range -\/1 to 1 with deadband already accounted for. Positive Y is towards the left wall when looking through the driver station glass. \\
\hline
{\em heading\+Adjust} & Double\+Supplier that supplies the component of the robot\textquotesingle{}s heading angle that should be adjusted. Should range from -\/1 to 1 with deadband already accounted for. \\
\hline
{\em look\+Away} & Face the robot towards the opposing alliance\textquotesingle{}s wall in the same direction the driver is facing \\
\hline
{\em look\+Towards} & Face the robot towards the driver \\
\hline
{\em look\+Left} & Face the robot left \\
\hline
{\em look\+Right} & Face the robot right \\
